### Expected Results
Checks #&#x2060;1, #&#x2060;2, and #&#x2060;3 are false.
### Procedures
 1. Check that [heading tags](https://pdfa.org/glossary-of-accessibility-terminology-in-pdf/#Heading_tag) are not used when the [author does not intend](https://pdfa.org/glossary-of-accessibility-terminology-in-pdf/#Authors_intent) a heading.
 2. Check that the heading levels are appropriate to the content hierarchy of the document.
 3. Check that, if the document's [title](https://pdfa.org/glossary-of-accessibility-terminology-in-pdf/#title-onpage) is tagged as (or [role mapped](https://pdfa.org/glossary-of-accessibility-terminology-in-pdf/#role-mapping) to) **H1**, the main section headings are tagged as (or [role mapped](https://pdfa.org/glossary-of-accessibility-terminology-in-pdf/#role-mapping) to) **H2**.
